The Mission

Founded in 2026, NEPAL.RAW was born out of a desire to move away from "fast media." We believe that the culture of Nepal and the Himalayas deserves more than just a 15-second social media clip. We focus on long-form storytelling that honors the history and the heartbeat of the land.


Our team spent 14 months trekking through the restricted zones of Upper Mustang and Dolpa to bring back stories that have never been told in the digital age. We document the granular details: the way the light hits a 12th-century monastery, the calloused hands of a mountain guide, and the silent retreat of a glacier.
